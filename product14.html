PINEAPPLE Soap

IPicture a luxurious bar of pineapple soap, infused with the essence of tropical paradise. As you lather it in your hands, the invigorating scent of fresh pineapple fills the air, transporting you to sun-drenched shores and swaying palm trees.

RECIPE

  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1 cup clear glycerin soap base, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon pineapple fragrance oil
  • Yellow soap colorant (optional)
  • Dried pineapple pieces (optional, for decoration)
  • In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the coconut oil in short bursts until fully liquefied.
  • Add the cubed glycerin soap base to the melted coconut oil.
  • Microwave the mixture in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the soap base is completely melted and well combined with the coconut oil.
  • Once melted, stir in the pineapple fragrance oil. If desired, add a few drops of yellow soap colorant to achieve a pineapple-like hue.
  • Pour the soap mixture into soap molds. If desired, place a few dried pineapple pieces into each mold for decoration.
  • Allow the soap to cool and harden at room temperature for several hours or until completely solid.
  • Once hardened, carefully remove the pineapple soap from the molds.
  • Your homemade Pineapple Soap is now ready to use! Enjoy the tropical fragrance and skin-nourishing properties of this delightful handmade soap.
